📄️ Read with Komic

Komic is an elegant, feature-rich iOS app designed to give you the best reading experience with your Komga server.

📄️ Read with MangaBox

MangaBox is a streamlined, multiplatform Komga client. It's free and open-source, available for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS/ipadOS and Android. It is based on web technologies so you can also deploy it as a web client or PWA.

📄️ Leer con KMReader

KMReader es un Cliente nativo de iOS, macOS, y tvOS para Komga, que incluye potentes lectores como DIVINA y EPUB, un almacén multiservidor, acceso sin conexión similar al de una unidad en la nube, paneles de control adaptativos y herramientas de administración con métricas en tiempo real a través de SSE.

📄️ Read with Yomu

Komga is supported natively in Yomu, a free manga, webtoon, and manhwa reader for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. No separate extension or repository to install, Komga support is built in.
